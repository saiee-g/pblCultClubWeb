About Us

PREC cultural is one of the largest and liveliest club of PREC; our philosophy is "Creativity brings courage" We aim to develope and promote arts and culture as a get way to personal development and social growth. The team takes part in various competitions at university level, Maharashtra as well as pan-India. It encompasses mainly Music,Dance,Arts and Crafts along with an extreamly creative backstage team . The cultural team is not just a club but a family with its own value systems that run on love, harmony and respect for each other.
